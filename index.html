Here's a fun fact about geese: There are actually different names for geese, another type of bird that can go in water.
Goose is actually a term for female geese, and male geese are called ganders. A group of geese on land or in water
are called a gaggle, and while they are in the air they are called a skein. Interesting!

Here is a list of geese breeds

  • Adler goose
  • American buff goose
  • Danish landrace goose
  • Emden goose

Here is a fun fact about each geese breed listed

  1. Adler geese rarely get sick and resists pests.
  2. American buff geese are the largest of the medium weight class of geese.
  3. Danish landrace geese are considered hardy birds that produce a low-fat carcass with a lot of meat. (whaaat)
  4. Emden geese is the only geese breed that is grown commercially for meat in North America due to its large size (awh)
